1.Each team will get 2 minutes to perform.
2.One player of the team has to put the card’s back against his/her forehead and the player standing opposite to him needs to describe the word written on the card by acting it out or by drawing it.
3.In the time allotted, the goal of the team is to guess maximum words. For each correct answer, the team will get 10 points. There is no negative marking.
4.The player describing the word is not allowed to speak or lip-sync. If this rule is violated 5 marks will be deducted from the total score.
5.If any of the players wants to skip the word just say “SKIP” and move to the next word. Any number of skips are allowed. Once you skip the word you cannot use that word again.
